Biryani Cafe

Here at Biryani cafe, we like to ensure your best meal possible along with the best experience possible.

Below, you can explore what we are all about, from how we started to our current endeavours.

When We Started


We started practicing this culinary art on November 14, 1979. Our founder, Charvik Penjarla I, wanted to provide the best vegetarian meals possible when he got the inspiration of cooking from his mother. Since 1979, Biryani Cafe has sought to ensure everyone who comes to this cafe gets treated like royalty.


Our Recipes


Below are some of our prestigous recipes on our menu for you to admire. We hope you will come to our restaurant to try them!


Paneer Tikka Masala

One of our original recipes, this curry used to be our main side offered since 1979. This recipe was praised by culinary artists such as Sanjeev Kapoor and Jiggs Kalra.
