When We Started


We started practicing this culinary art on November 14, 1979. Our founder, Charvik Penjarla I, wanted to provide the best vegetarian meals possible when he got the inspiration of cooking from his mother. Since 1979, Biryani Cafe has sought to ensure everyone who comes to this cafe gets treated like royalty.
